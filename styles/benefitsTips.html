Benefits and Tips

Some benefits and tips of coffee

  1. What are the benefits of drink coffee?
    • Caffeine is the component that simulates and activates us.
    • Coffee improves your mood.
    • Coffee increases your physical performance.

  1. Tips to enjoy coffee.
    • Take in moderation.
    • If you use a coffee maker and peper filters, moisten the coffee with cold water before putting on the hot water.
    • It is recommended to grind it before preparing it, to prevent it from oxidizing.
